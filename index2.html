Company History

Four Decades of
Powering Gujarat

From a single contracting firm to a fully integrated electrical infrastructure company — our story spans over 45 years of consistent growth, technical excellence, and client trust.

1980
Established as Pandya Brothers
Founded as a small electrical contracting firm in Gujarat with a vision to deliver quality electrical infrastructure services to government and private clients.
2010
Expanded via Dalmi Enterprise
Expanded capabilities and operational reach by establishing Dalmi Enterprise, a sister firm focused on specialized electrical works.
2025
Pandya Brothers Electropower Pvt. Ltd.
Pandya Brothers and Dalmi Enterprise successfully merged to form the newly rebranded Pandya Brothers Electropower Pvt. Ltd. — a stronger, unified entity.

What We Do

Core Services

Comprehensive electrical infrastructure solutions built on decades of experience.

01 —

Transmission Line Erection

Survey, foundation, erection, stringing & commissioning of 66kV to 220kV lines with full safety compliance.

02 —

Underground EHV Cabling

Cable laying, testing & commissioning for 66kV to 220kV systems including HDD, excavation and jointing works.

03 —

HV / LV Switchgear

Erection, testing and commissioning of high-voltage and low-voltage switchgear systems.

04 —

Civil & Substation Works

Substation foundation, structure fabrication, transformer pads, cable trenching & infrastructure support.

05 —

Street & Industrial Lighting

Street lighting, feeder pillars, and industrial lighting solutions with energy-efficient technology.

06 —

Building Electrification

Complete building wiring, fire safety equipment, distribution boards and control panel installation.

07 —

Annual Maintenance & AMC

Annual maintenance contracts, technical servicing and complete system overhauling for uninterrupted operations.

08 —

Distribution & Control Panels

Design, installation and testing of distribution and control panels for industrial and commercial applications.
