What We Do

Our Core Services

Comprehensive electrical infrastructure solutions for government and private sector projects across Gujarat and beyond.

Transmission Line Erection

Survey, foundation, erection, stringing, and commissioning of transmission lines from 66kV to 220kV with full safety compliance.

Underground EHV Cable Laying

Underground EHV cable laying, testing, and commissioning for 66kV to 220kV cabling systems including HDD and excavation works.

HV & LV Switchgear Systems

Erection, testing, and commissioning of high-voltage and low-voltage switchgear systems with precision and safety.

Civil & Substation Works

Comprehensive civil works including substation foundation, equipment structure fabrication, transformer foundations, and cable trenching.

Street & Industrial Lighting

Street lighting, feeder pillars, and industrial lighting solutions with energy-efficient modern technology.

Building Electrification

Complete building electrification including fire safety equipment installation, distribution and control panel setup.

Annual Maintenance & Overhauling

Annual maintenance contracts, technical servicing, and complete system overhauling to ensure continuous operational reliability.

Distribution & Control Panels

Design, installation, and testing of distribution and control panels for industrial and commercial applications.
